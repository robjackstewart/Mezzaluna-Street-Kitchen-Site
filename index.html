About

Who We Are

Mezzaluna Street Kitchen is a Dundee based healthy food-to-go kitchen with a difference. Our ethos 'Italian born, Dundee bread' flies the flag for local usage of products regardless of where you are from.

Whether you are vegan or, vegetarian, a carnivore or gluten free, MSK serves up quality dishes at affordable prices.

Flavour First

Taste is everything: for our salads, it’s all in the herbs and dressings; for our meats it’s all in the seasoning and stock; for everything else it’s the quality of the products we source.

Health & Nutrition

Without trying too hard (steering clear of faddy terms like ‘clean eating', ‘raw’ and ‘paleo’ diets), we seek to demonstrate that simple everyday food can easily be transformed into something tasty, healthy and good for your body.

Suppliers

Being a mobile business, we source exclusively from local businesses surrounding the event we are preparing for. With our customers in mind, we aim to bring you fresh, nutritious and most importantly delicious food, while showcasing the amazing talents of our partners.

restaurant_menuOur Food

Vegan

With few exceptions our daily three-salad selection are almost always vegan. We offer vegan alternative spreads in our sandwiches and wraps. Our daily soup is often but not always vegan. We have vegan cakes and offer a dairy alternative to milk for your flat white! There’s always a tasty option for the vegan diet on the QSK menu.

Vegetarian

Take the information in our vegan section and add cheese, grilled, grated or melted!

Carnivore

We slow cook our meats daily, whether it’s slow cooked Welsh lamb, perfectly pulled pork, or beautifully braised beef brisket – they’re served hot on top of the QSK salad box, or in a baguette, sandwich or wrap.

Gluten Free

We think about the gluten free element in all our preparations. While we cannot guarantee there’s no cross contamination, we take care to use gluten free products in our dressings and stocks so those that have a GF diet can enjoy our slow cooked meats, our soups and our salads. We make our salads with GF grains and pulses, including quinoa, lentils and chickpeas. We offer gluten free bread and wraps and have a broad selection of tasty GF cakes.

Contact

If you have any questions you are very welcome to contact us.

We are available for festivals, local markets and private events.
